## When to invoke the Bharat AI Mission alignment

The Bharat AI Mission link is a credibility marker for India-focused audiences and a credible motivation for the global-south wedge. **Do not lead a US-focused YC investor email with it.** Use it when:

- The audience is India- or Asia-focused (Indian VCs, regional press, Indian conferences).
- The question is about *why this market* or *why this wedge* — Bharat AI Mission alignment is a defensible answer for "why are you starting in India and SEA / MENA / LATAM at low price points."
- The surface explicitly asks about company values or social mission.

**Do not** invoke it as the primary differentiator with a US YC partner — they need the engineering and insight story first; "social mission" is a bonus, not the lead.
